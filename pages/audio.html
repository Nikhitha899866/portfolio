Sound Story

In a crowded bar, a man and his friends are drinking and laughing. The man wants to leave, and he says, "See you later." Another person in the group says, "Bye," but in a funny voice, which makes everyone laugh again. This person leaves the place and whistles as he walks to his car. He opens the door to his car and after sitting he close the door. He is drunk and didn't put his seat belt on before starting the car . After he starts the car, his phone rings, and he observes it instead of at the road. This causes the car to skid first and due to hand break fails the car crashes. People near by rush to help him because the person got hurt and moaning on the inside. Right away, the police and an ambulance arrive. The person will have a hard time breathing, and they will take him into ambulance and starts minor treatment , but his heartbeat rises up, and he cant able to breath and respond to treatment, the ECG monitor sounds that the heart beat has been stoped and he passes away in the ambulance itself.

Processing of Audio Effects :

  1. First music > cropped and generated silent for remaining music > Applied Fade in & Fade out > kept in separate channel.
  2. Cropped and merged restaurant sounds > fade in > fade out > envelop affect applied in middle > Arranged in different channels.
  3. Increased and decreased volume > Particularly in different channels> Trim outside selection > volume adjustments > copy and paste to separate channel.
  4. Most of the Sounds merged > Generated Silence > Pitch Adjustments > Noise Reduction > Arranged in Channels.

Description of Audio Editing Process :

  1. First, I picked some happy music, cut out the part I wanted, and added fade-in and fade-out effects to the beginning and end.
  2. Once I've gotten all the sounds I need for a bar, like the sound of a bottle being opened, people laughing, the sound of drinking alcohol, and some background laughter, I'll placed them in and arranged all together.
  3. I kept them on three separate channels and set them up so that they sound good together.I applied silence for unrequired sound to stop when it wasn't needed, added pitch and tempo effects, and changed them as needed.
  4. Again, I've recorded & collected some different sounds, such as a person walking and whistling, a car door opening and closing, a ringtone, and a hand break.
  5. I also put this in different channels and took out some sounds I didn't like, merged them together, and used the envelop tool, which is needed for some high-rise sounds.
  6. The rest of the car crash sounds have been edited and mixed together, and the sounds of the ambulance and heartbeat have been put on different channels,to show the different modules of sound given different effects, and changed the audio settings.
  7. Finally, I went through all of the sounds and made different changes to them. I also moved some sounds to different channels, where I edited the sound modes to make the left and right sounds louder and quieter, and added the necessary effects.
